#### UI Shortcuts
- **Hide the side bar** = CTRL+B
- **Hide activity bar** = ALT+I
- **Hide line numbers** = ALT+L
- **Hide tab bar** = ALT+T

#### Editor Shortcuts
- **Wrap the words to the view** = ALT+Z
